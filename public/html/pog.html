Pirate Prowess!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four pirate items. By clicking on a pirate item, you will add a certain number of pirate-points to your score.

You win the game by matching your pirate-points to the random number provided.

The number of pirate-points alotted to each pog varies from pirate item to pirate item and is not displayed, you'll have to figure out how many pirate-points each pirate item is worth by clicking the pirate item.

Each time you start a new game, the pirate-points will vary.

Good luck, and may the strongest swashbuckler win!
